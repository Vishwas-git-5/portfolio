Movie Recommendations

Top 3 movies of all time

Interstellar

Interstellar is a beautiful representation of the cosmos,
along with the element of love added as a natural instinct of
human race to the science that tries to explain the dimensions.

The Dark Knight Rises

Good and bad are not the only two sides of a coin,
there's a third side that represents both, this is
shown as Batman, a self imposed hero tries to fight evil.

Avengers Infinity War

When a mad titan tries to help the universe by killing
half of the world's population, the avengers must fight
him, to save the other half.
